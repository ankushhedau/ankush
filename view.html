About Me

Professional Summary

I am working as National Post-Doctoral Fellow (ANRF-NPDF) at IIT Hyderabad, developing PCM-based wearable cooling vests with multi-physics CFD simulations, bio-mimetic experimental testing, and thermoregulation modeling to prevent heatstroke in extreme Indian summer conditions (40-55¬∞C, 40-90% RH). During my PhD at IIT Roorkee as Prime Minister Research Fellow, I conducted pioneering research on corrugated double-pass solar air heaters integrated with PCM thermal energy storage, publishing 7 SCI papers and developing validated Nusselt number/friction factor correlations. Previously at IIT Delhi as Senior Research Fellow, I optimized sustainable building designs for Himalayan regions using genetic algorithms, EnergyPlus, and DesignBuilder for passive strategies and PCM integration. At IIT Bombay as a Post-doctoral Fellow, I investigated heat transfer enhancement in rotating pin-fin ducts for gas turbine cooling applications using experimental analysis and advanced ANSYS Fluent turbulence modelling.

Research

Current Research Project

Development and Integration of PCM-based Cooling Vests for Heatstroke Prevention in India's Extreme Weather Conditions

Institution: IIT Hyderabad
Duration: December 2025 - Present
Funding: ANRF (PDF/2025/000302)

This National Post-Doctoral Fellowship project addresses a critical public health challenge in India - heatstroke prevention in extreme weather conditions (40-55¬∞C, 40-90% humidity). The research integrates thermal engineering, materials science, and human physiology to develop wearable cooling technology.

Key Research Components:

  • Multi-physics CFD Simulations: Phase change material thermal modeling, heat transfer within cooling vest channels, human thermoregulation models
  • Bio-mimetic Thermal Manikin Testing: Custom-fabricated torso thermal manikin with multi-zone heating, integrated sweating simulation system, real-time physiological sensor integration
  • Thermoregulation Modeling: Predictive thermoregulation-behavioral adaptation models, real-time heatstroke risk assessment algorithms
